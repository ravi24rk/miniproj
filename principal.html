PRINCIPAL'S MESSAGE:




"The highest education is that which does not merely give us information but makes our life in harmony with all existence". - Rabindranath Tagore

The high quality infrastructure, well qualified faculty members, well equipped laboratories, modern library facilities, facilities for sports & games and co-curricular activities, coupled with student centric teaching-learning process assist the students to choose and achieve what they want. It is certainly a matter of great satisfaction and pride for me to note that significant improvements have been made in the sphere of imparting most modern higher education in our college.

We are dedicated towards providing excellent higher education, to mould the students as global professionals who will be highly competent in their chosen fields, ethical in behavior, with an curious mind, an appreciation for good values in life, and with a commitment to serve the society. Government College of Engineering, Bodinayakkanur strives to make a high valued, intellectual and ethical educational service to the society.

Quality comes as a consequence of strong intentions, intelligent decisions, sincere effort and skillful executions. Being one of the youngest government engineering institutions in Tamilnadu state of India, we make every effort for the excellence in every step.

Dr.C.Vasanthanayaki.,M.E., Ph.D.,
