OLD BOYS ASSOCIATION

There is an Old Students Association in the college and all out going students become members of the association. The general body meets on first Sunday of every August. The executive committee meets frequently. The association is offering financial and Technical assistance for the development of the Institution.
The association is proud to see many of its members serving most of the countries in the world. The Principal and the mangement are very glad at the outstanding performance of the alumni.


The association appreciates all the alumni who have taken their efforts for serving the Placement cell of our college. The association is thankful to them and others who have helped the college flag fly high by visiting the almamater for campus interviews and seminars.


The association gives loan to the needy students based on the rules and regulations form Alumni Association Financial aid fund. The association appreciates the members for the valuable donation for this financial aid fund.
