MESS TIMINGS:



There will be a separate mess for boys and girls and it is attached to their respective Hostel. Meals will be served in the dining hall at the following hour:



Breakfast: 7.00 a.m - 9.00 a.m



Lunch: 12.30 p.m - 2.00 p.m



Evening Tea: 4.30 p.m - 5.30 p.m



Dinner: 7.30 p.m - 9.00 p.m


Change over from Veg. mess to Non-Veg. mess or vice versa will be permitted for a minimum of one calendar month with prior written approval of the warden.



Reduction in mess bill on account of absence from mess will be allowed only if prior permission in writing has been obtained from the warden-in-charge of the mess and handed over in advance to the mess managers. The concession in variable charges is considered only if the student is away from the campus and will be allowed only if the absence is and will be allowed only if the absence is between 4 to 7 days……50% concession , 8 days or above…..100% Concession



However students are required to bear Rs.200/- per month fixed charges. Any discrepancies in the monthly and cumulative mess charges displayed in the notice boards should be intimated in the office within seven days of the notice.
